Healthcare

Medicare Doesn't Work Abroad — and the Penalty for Pausing It Is Permanent

Dropping Medicare Part B while abroad triggers a 10% premium surcharge for every 12-month period you weren't covered. It never goes away.

Typical cost: $1,200–$2,400/year permanently
Tax & Reporting

FBAR Applies to Every Foreign Account Over $10,000 — Most Retirees Don't Know Until It's Too Late

The $10,000 FBAR threshold is aggregate and based on peak balance, not year-end balance. Willful non-filing: 50% of account value per year.

Penalty range: $10K–$100K+ per year
Visa

The Portugal D7 Visa Rejects Applications Using Income Figures from Outdated Blogs

The minimum passive income threshold updates without fanfare. Applications using figures from 12-month-old expat blogs are rejected — and fees are non-refundable.

Wasted: $800–$2,500 per failed attempt
