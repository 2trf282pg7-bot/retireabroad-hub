How Commissions Work

Commission structures vary by partner. Health insurance partners typically pay a commission on the first year's premium when a policy is purchased through a referral link. Banking and transfer partners typically pay a flat referral fee or a small percentage of transferred amounts. Tax service partners pay a flat fee per qualified referral.

We do not receive higher commissions for ranking one insurer above another. Rankings and recommendations reflect expat community feedback, claims payment records, and policy terms — not commission rates.
